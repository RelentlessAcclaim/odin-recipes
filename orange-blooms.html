Orange Blooms

Description

Orange blooms are perfect anytime of the year and easy to make!

Ingredients

Cupcakes:

  • 1 18.25 oz. yellow cake mix (don't use a pudding mix)
  • 1 3.4 oz. instant lemon pudding
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 c. orange juice
  • 3/4 c. oil

Glaze:

  • 2 Tbsp. oil
  • 1.3 c. + 2 Tbsp. orange juice
  • 3 c. powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients and pour into mini-muffin pans, that have been sprayed with non-stick spray with flour.
  2. Bake in preheated 350Â° oven for 7 to 10 minutes
  3. Glaze:Mix all nigredients until smooth.
  4. Dip cupcakes in glaze and set on rack to cool
