Cast Iron Ribeye

Description

Just because you don't have a grill doesn't mean you can't enjoy a restaurant quality steak at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 Ribeye
  • Kosher Salt
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat cast iron skillet on medium heat. Add high smoking temp oil after a few minutes. Pan is ready once it's smoking.
  2. Cook steak on every side for 1-2 minutes each
  3. Let steak rest for 5 minutes and enjoy!
